Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I save the greeting cards I created? After finishing your design in "Create New Card" or "Edit Template", tap the Save/Download icon. Please make sure you have allowed the app to access your Photo Library.
2. Can I customize the Lucky Wheel? Yes! Go to the Lucky Wheel feature, you can edit the text for each segment of the wheel to suit your party or family gathering.
3. Why can't I upload my photo to the template? Please check your device Settings > Privacy > Photos and ensure that Chúc Tết has "All Photos" or "Selected Photos" access.
4. Are the recipes and Tet customs content updated? We regularly update the content to ensure accuracy for each Lunar New Year. If you find any incorrect information, please let us know.
5. Is my data stored on a server? No. Most of your data (custom Lucky Wheel items, edited photos) is stored locally on your device to protect your privacy.

Technical Support

For crash reports or bugs, please include the following in your email for faster support:

  • Your device model (e.g., iPhone 15 Pro)
  • iOS version (e.g., iOS 17.2)
  • A brief description of what happened before the crash.
