This usually happens if the app doesn't have permission to access your Photo Library. Please go to your iPhone Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos, find "Chúc Tết 2026", and select "Add Photos Only" or "Full Access". Then try saving again.

Currently, the app focuses on providing high-quality designed templates where you can customize wishes and text. We do not support uploading personal photos onto the cards in this version.
